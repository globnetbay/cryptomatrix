During registration, I was asked to provide the Bitcoin address. What is this?
Bitcoin address is your ID (account, wallet number), starting with 1 or 3 and containing 27-34 alphanumeric Latin characters (other than 0, O, I). The address can also be represented as a QR-code, it is anonymous and does not contain information about the owner.
